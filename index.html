Theme days 2021!

What is a theme day? During each two-week session, we will have theme days where campers and staff dress according to a specific theme. There will be costume contests, themed events for the day, and other fun and themed surprises. While participation in theme days is optional, we highly encourage our campers to participate for the most camp fun!

BIGGEST Event of the Year-Open House!

It’s our favorite time of the year…well, our favorite time besides SUMMER, of course! We love getting to see so many of our summer camp families and summer camp counselors at our Southern California Open House & Reunion in Calabasas, California on January 26th, 2020 from 2-5 pm.
